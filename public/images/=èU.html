{ 02. Frequently Asked Questions }

Got a few more questions?

Do I need my credit card details to sign up?

No need at all!

We only need your email address and name, and then you can start to send emails directly from JavaScript.

Can I use EmailJS for free?

Absolutely!

You can send up to 200 emails per month completely for free. If you need to send more emails, want to use advanced features like file attachments or remove our footer from the emails – you may upgrade your plan.

What if I change my mind?

You can cancel or change your plan at any time directly from your account. Your data will be stored if you wish to use our services again, or we can delete all of your records upon your request.

Are there any contracts?

There are no contracts or hidden fees with EmailJS. You may change plans or cancel at any time.

How can I pay?

All payments are made by credit card, through FastSpring secure payment processor or by Paypal. If you subscribe to a monthly plan, the subscription period is one month and charges are processed of each period. All statements are available for your account.
